# Hasan Aspendos

Aspendos Antique Theatre

Aspendos Antique Theatre A stark comparison from the modernity of the Antalya city center is the Ancient City of Aspendos, found 25 miles to the east.

Records hold that Aspendos’ history goes back as far as 1000 BC and flourishing in the 5th century BC, where it became a center of trade and industry with its abundance of oil, wool, and salt.

Local architect Zenon under the rule of Marcus Aurelius built this masterpiece in 155 AD. It was a present to the emperor and to the Roman gods and the city derived great wealth from a trade in salt, oil and wool.
